2023

SFSCON 2023 Hackathon - Winner Team

Duration: 24 hours
Infominds AG/SPA

Developed an infrastructure that connects IoT devices with a web service through a Raspberry Pi gateway and custom APIs. A machine learning model is used to forecast the energy consumption of the building and to optimize the energy usage.

Hackathon NOI Summer 2023 - Winner Team

Duration: 24 hours
Matrycs European Project

Data analysis of a environmental dataset, from the data understanding to outlier removal and statistical evaluations. Developed a forecasting model (LSTM) for time-series analysis and validated the process. We presented our solution at the Matrycs European Project launch event in Brussels, Belgium.

Start-Up Lab - Third Place

Duration: 3 months

3 months course on developing a business starting from an idea and following the LEAN startup principles. Developed a physical MVP which we used to receive feedback from potential customers. Chosen among other 20 business ideas, we won an entrance ticket to an important startup event in Milan, Italy.

2022

Industrial AI Challenge HIT - Second Place

Duration: 3 months
Dana Incorporated

Evaluated the performance of different machine learning models for predicting the results of quality tests related to driveline mechanical components. Developed a desktop app to gather data from the sensors and provide the predictions to the operator in real-time.

PROTO Challenge 2022 HIT - Winner Team

Duration: 3 months
Oversonic Robotics

Analyzed the leg of a humanoid robot to increase its degree of freedom and allow it to squat asynchronously. Designed and topology-optimized a prototype of the leg to be manufactured with additive manufacturing techniques.

2015

International Year of Light - Second Place

Designed and built a "smart flower" that moves to follow the sun and closes its petals when the weather is bad to reduce cleaning costs. Most of the components were 3D printed and an Arduino Nano was used to control the system.
